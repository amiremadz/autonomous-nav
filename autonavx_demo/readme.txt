o start the simulation click on the play button in the lower right corner. It will run for ever. To stop the simulation use the stop button right next to the play button. Once the simulation is running it will show the simulation time in seconds in the top right corner.

Camera Control

Hold the left mouse button to rotate the camera
Hold the right mouse button to move the camera around
Use the scroll wheel to zoom in and out
Quadrotor Control

W & S keys control the pitch angle
A & D keys control the roll angle
Q & E keys control the yaw angle
Up & Down keys control the vertical velocity along the Z axes
